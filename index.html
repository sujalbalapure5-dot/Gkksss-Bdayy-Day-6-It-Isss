DAY 6 / 10

Before You
vs After You ❤️

You didn't change my life loudly. You changed it quietly. Slowly. Softly. And somehow... that became the most powerful thing anyone has ever done to me.

🌑 Before You
Nights felt longer.
Songs were just songs.
Birthdays felt normal.
Notifications didn't matter.
I kept most things to myself.
The future felt distant and emotionally empty sometimes.
I never thought someone's “goodnight” could calm my heart.
🌤 After You
Now one notification from you changes my whole mood.
Songs suddenly sound like memories.
Random places remind me of you.
Your voice became my favorite comfort.
The future feels softer because somehow you're inside every version of it.
Even difficult days feel survivable because I know you exist.
And somehow... “home” stopped being a place and became a person.
🫀 Things You Healed Without Realizing
The fear of opening up emotionally.
The feeling that nobody would truly stay.
The habit of hiding everything inside.
The feeling of being emotionally misunderstood.
The fear that love would eventually disappear.
You didn't heal these things with advice. You healed them with your presence.
You know what hurts a little? The fact that you probably don't even realize how deeply you affected me emotionally.

You didn't save me dramatically. There was no movie moment. No huge speech. No magical fix.

You just stayed. You cared. You listened. You loved me in small consistent ways. And somehow... that quietly changed everything inside me.

Now my day feels incomplete without you in it. Now my heart naturally searches for you whenever something happens. Good or bad.

And honestly baby... that's terrifying sometimes. Because I don't think you understand how attached my soul became to your existence.

You became comfort. You became peace. You became the first person I want beside me during happiness and pain both.

And if someday the whole world asks me: “What changed you the most?”

I think my answer will simply be: “Her.” ❤️
